Thesis and Publication

Analysis of Stock Market Forecasting Utilizing an LSTM Approach
Undergraduate Thesis
2023

My thesis involved analyzing the performance of a Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) model using data from the Dhaka Stock Exchange (DSE). Utilizing stock data from five different companies of DSE across distinct categories, I conducted a comparative analysis against the NASDAQ Stock Exchange. The results demonstrated superior accuracy of the N ASDAQ data with the RNN model, highlighting the heightened uncertainty prevalent in the Dhaka Stock Exchange.
